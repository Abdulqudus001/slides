Accessibility

AKA (A11Y), means making sure products, or solutions are usable by everyone regardless of what disability they have or condition they're in

Why it matters

According to the World Health Organization (WHO), an estimated 1.3 billion people (>15%) of the world's population live with disability

Colors & Accessibility

Accessible colors

Color combinations that have enough contrast to make elements clearly distinguishable to those visually impaired or deficient in color vision

Color contrast ranges from 1 - 21, with the minimun acceptable contrast ratio being 4.5:1

Black text on white text

Contrast == 21

Light red text on black background

Contrast == 5.58

Light red text on green background

Contrast == 1.99
